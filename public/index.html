Marion Harding 2015 - Beyond

The 2015 season was a record-shattering season for the Marion Harding Marching Band. In addition to recieving “Grand Champion” at every regular competition they attended, and recieving a “Superior” rating at OMEA State Competition, the 2015 Marion Harding Marching Band competed at the Outback Bowl competition and won the opportunity to perform during halftime of the nationally-televised 2016 Outback Bowl. 2015 was a truly undefeated season for this remarkable band.

  • Client: Marion Harding High School, OH
  • Date: Fall 2015
  • Category: Drill & Visual Design

Marion Harding 2014 - Valor

The 2014 season for the Marion Harding Marching Band was a special one, also it was the first show that Nick and Tim worked together on a program. Revamping the color guard program was a key goal this year, and it has set the stage for this school’s future success. Combined with some stunning visual effects, Marion Harding Marching Band recieved a “Superior” rating at OMEA State Competition.

  • Client: Marion Harding High School, OH
  • Date: Fall 2014
  • Category: Drill & Visual Design
